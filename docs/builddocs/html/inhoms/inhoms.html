Polygonal inhomogeneities can be added in three ways:

1. PolygonInhomMaq, which is an inhomogeneity consisting of a regular sequence of aquifer - leaky layer - aquifer - leaky layer, aquifer, etc. The top of the system can be either an aquifer or a leaky layer.

2. PolygonInhom3D, which is an inhomogeneity consisting of a stack of aquifer layers. The resistance between the aquifer layers is computed as the resistance from the middle of one layer to the middle of the next layer. Vertical anisotropy can be specified. The system may be bounded on top by a leaky layer.
